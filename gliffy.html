Gliffy

Gliffy is a small B2B tech company that makes a diagramming web app used by thousands of software companies. During my time there, I worked on all types of design projects ranging from new product features, growth hacking designs, marketing initiatives, and user research. I was responsible for carrying out the design process from start to finish - from initial concepts to finished visual design assets.

Commenting feature

The concept of this came out of a series interviews we did with our users on how we could improve collaboration for them on Gliffy. After extensive product concept testing and validation with Gliffy users, I designed a comprehensive system for users to be able to comment on their diagrams directly and have conversations with their teammates. I prototyped and tested mockups with users via TryMyUI.com to ensure quality usability standards.

Growth hack designs

Gliffy set a goal to increase the number of teams using our product. I was the main designer helping our growth hack team design for quick A/B tests to increase conversion. This included reworking our pricing page and purchase flow.

Core values

I worked with Gliffy's CEO and copywriter to come up with a set of visuals that embodied the company's core values. I revamped the design for Gliffy's mascot Djedi and painted the illustrations in Paper 53. They were printed as large wall decals and on various pieces of merchandise.

Steampunk bar

Gliffy had a team bonding event where we built a bar together at a metal smithing school. I designed the originial concept for the bar and worked with some of the instructors of the school on its construction plan. The bar houses two beer kegs and wine.
