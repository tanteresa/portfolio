Kabam

Kabam is a large mobile games developer and publisher based in San Francisco. I was one of the few UX designers working in their entire company and established UX best practices for my teams. I created wireframes and prototypes for 7 web and mobile games. I also conducted usability testing for new designs and collaborated with game producers, engineers, and UI artists.
