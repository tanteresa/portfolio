St. Luke's health system

www.stlukesonline.org
Case study on extractable.com

St. Luke's is an Idaho-based, non-profit health system made up of numerous hospitals and clinics all throughout the state. The original website was huge with many separate clinic websites linked together. Our goal was to redesign their website in a way that enables St. Lukes to provide the best healthcare possible to their community. This included a major restructuring of their information architecture, an improved Doctor's Finder page, a system to clearly highlight health education articles, and a fully responsive design.

I was one of two UX designers on the project along with a couple of content strategists and a small visual design team at Extractable. I made hundreds of wireframes/prototypes and UX documentation over the course of 2 months. I also helped moderate live user tests with actual patients in Idaho.

Micron

www.micron.com
Case study on extractable.com

Micron is a major electronics memory and storage manufacturer based in Boise, Idaho. Our goal was give their company website a modern face lift while streamlining the information architecture to better serve their customers. I was mainly responsible for the redesign of their About page and helped Micron communicate its story in a more compelling manner.

I was one of two UX designers on the project along with a couple of content strategists and a small visual design team at Extractable. I made hundreds of wireframes and prototypes over the course of 2 months.

Desert Schools Credit Union

www.desertschools.com/

Desert Schools (now Desert Financial) is the largest Arizona-based credit union that needed a major redesign of its website. I wireframed and prototyped the new website experience along with the mobile version.

I was the sole UX designer on this project and collaborated with one other content strategist and visual designer at Extractable.
